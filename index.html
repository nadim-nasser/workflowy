What's in the AI Readiness Audit?

A diagnostic that measures both your people and your organization so you invest in the right interventions.

Workforce AI Readiness

How ready are your people to use AI effectively?

We assess your team's ability to apply AI in real work, not just their familiarity with the tools.

Ability to Apply AI

"Can employees identify opportunities to use AI in their daily work?"

AI Knowledge & Skills

"Do team members understand how to prompt, iterate, and validate AI outputs?"

AI Openness

"Are employees willing to experiment with AI, or do they see it as a threat?"

AI Risks & Ethics

"Do employees recognize AI limitations and use it responsibly?"

Scorecard Output: Each employee receives a readiness score (Basic, Intermediate, Advanced, or Expert) with specific skill gaps identified and recommended learning paths.

Organizational AI Readiness

What enables or blocks AI adoption at scale?

We evaluate the structural factors that determine whether AI can actually succeed in your environment.

Strategy & Leadership

"Do leaders share a clear, measurable AI vision and actively steer the organization in the same direction?"

Data Readiness

"How reliable, accessible, and usable is your data? AI is only as good as the information it's built on."

Systems & Infrastructure

"Does your technical foundation allow AI to run smoothly, integrate with workflows, and scale?"

Governance & Compliance

"Are there clear policies for AI use, data privacy, and ethical oversight?"

Culture & Change Readiness

"Is the organization open to new ways of working, or will AI face resistance?"

Scorecard Output: A comprehensive alignment report showing where your organization stands (At Risk, Fragmented, Developing, or Aligned) with prioritized recommendations for each dimension.
