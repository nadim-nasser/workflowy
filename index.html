The problem

Most AI training ends with a slide deck.

  • × Generic prompting workshops that don't stick
  • × No connection to your actual workflows or data
  • × No clear path from training day to daily adoption
  • × Teams go back to doing things the old way

Our approach

Yours ends with working prototypes and a 90-day playbook.

  • Hands-on labs with your real data and tools
  • Strategic discussion on what AI adoption actually takes
  • Custom AI toolkit your team keeps forever
  • Department-level 30/60/90 day action plan
The Day

From strategy to building to next steps.

A full-day executive session designed around your organization. Not a lecture. A working session where your team builds real things.

Step 01

9:00 AM

Opening & Assessment

Context setting, survey shareback, and an honest strategic discussion: What's been holding your team back from being AI-first?

Step 02

10:00 AM

AI Landscape & Vision

Where AI is today, the tool ecosystem, prompting best practices, and what it means for your organization over the next 12-18 months.

Step 03

11:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Hands-On Labs

Hands-On

The core of the day. Three rotations of building with real tools and real data:

Lab 1: Build

Presentations, dashboards, and data visualizations using Claude Desktop.

Lab 2: Code

Live-code a tool using Claude Code.

Lab 3: Automate

Map and build one workflow using n8n, Zapier, Claude, and Gemini.

Step 04

3:00 PM

Guardrails & Governance

What you can and can't do with your data. Compliance considerations, responsible AI use, and the practical tradeoffs between tools.

Step 05

3:30 PM

Strategy & Next Steps

Tactical planning by department. Quick wins, ownership assignments, and 30/60/90 day milestones to become AI-first.

What You Walk Away With

Every engagement produces tangible assets your team uses long after the session ends.

Executive AI Toolkit

A custom-built, password-protected training hub your team keeps forever. Not a slide deck. An interactive resource with prompting guides, labs, templates, and best practices tailored to your org.

AI Strategy Jamboard

A collaborative strategy board capturing team challenges, department-specific bets, success metrics, and the strategic decisions made during the session.

90-Day AI Adoption Playbook

A sprint-based adoption plan with ownership assignments, department priorities, and week-by-week milestones. Not theory. Executable next steps.

Ongoing Support

Recorded sessions, async support, and follow-up check-ins to make sure the momentum from the session translates into real organizational change.
