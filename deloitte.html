The Evolution

From chatbots to autonomous agents

Leading enterprises are already moving beyond chat. The question is: can your people keep up?

Stage 1

Chat Interface

Human at keyboard, getting answers. Reactive Q&A. No tool use.

Where most employees are today

Stage 2

Human-Guided Agents

Human guides, AI agent completes multi-step tasks. Uses tools + data.

Where leaders need to be

Stage 3

Autonomous Agents

AI acts independently, pulling humans in only for approvals and exceptions.

Where enterprises are headed

Major banks are already building agentic platforms. RBC, TD, and JP Morgan are deploying agent architectures with LLM gateways, human-in-the-loop controls, and agent marketplaces. The infrastructure exists. The skills don't.

The Skills Gap
